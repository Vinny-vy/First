    IPL Trophy

    The Indian Premier League (IPL) is a professional Twenty20 cricket league in India contested during March or April and May of every year by 8 teams representing 8 different cities of India. The league was founded by the Board of Control for Cricket in India (BCCI) in 2008. IPL has an exclusive window in ICC Future Tours Programme.
